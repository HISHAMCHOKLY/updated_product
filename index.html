Our Products

Sahab al Watan medical devices offer a diverse range of the latest, technologically advanced products and solutions of prominent quality to serve various medical sectors and healthcare facilities across the kingdom of Saudi Arabia and its friendly countries. Our company portfolio encompasses an array of medical, disposal, and surgical product offerings, and while our profile continues to grow, our current main areas of specialties are within the following domains:

Surgical

Surgical instruments are manufactured with the highest quality materials and craftsmanship to provide you with reliable instruments at a cost-effective price. We offer a wide variety of surgical tools, including surgical kits. As a major provider of surgical devices to the healthcare sector, we guarantee that every product is designed to function with dependable efficiency and comfort under all conditions.

Disposable

Disposable medical supplies prioritize infection prevention over other medical products, which is why they are becoming more and more important in Saudi Arabia and around the world. There are two categories of disposable medical equipment: that which is used in hospitals and used at home. Face masks, shoe coverings, dental supplies, bed sheet and pillow covers, surgical and patient gowns, infant gear, delivery mats, and other items are among the items. As a significant supplier of medical disposal goods, we ensure that every medical disposal device is manufactured to the highest standards of comfort and quality in all situations at affordable pricing

Equipments

We are recognized as one of the most reputable suppliers of top-notch medical equipment in Saudi Arabia and its neighbouring nations. Accurate measurements, top-notch performance, great usefulness, and durability are all qualities that our products are praised for. In the manufacturing facility of our suppliers, these items are created using premium raw materials and cutting-edge machinery. This equipment come in a huge range of specifications and may be tailored to meet the needs of the customers.
